## Advanced Editing Techniques That Bypass Detectors

Once your draft is complete, use these editing passes specifically targeting **AI detection** vulnerabilities.

*   **The 'Why' Pass:** For every key statement or claim, ask yourself "Why?" or "How do I know this?" and add that explanation. AI states facts; humans explain their reasoning.
*   **The Flow Disruption Pass:** Intentionally break up predictable flow patterns. Move a key point from the end of a paragraph to the beginning of the next. Insert a relevant but slightly tangential observation before returning to the main thread.
*   **The Sensory Detail Pass:** Where appropriate, engage senses beyond sight. Describe a sound, texture, or even a remembered smell related to your topic. This accesses uniquely human memory banks.
